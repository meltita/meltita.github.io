Expedited delivery of FCL and LCL-cargo from China by rail.

Railway is one of the most reliable means of transport. It may be used for transportation of all sorts of cargo at any weather conditions. Particularly attractive is railway transport for delivery of very big amounts of goods for very long distances.

We offer also delivery of smaller quantities of goods less expensive than airfreight and significantly faster than ocean freight. This is expedited delivery from China by rail in special container trains.

We consolidate different types of LCL-cargo at rail container terminals in following cities:

East-China: Shanghai, Ningbo, Suzhou, Kunshan, Changzhou, Ruian, Wuxi;
North-China: Tianjin, Qingdao, Beijing, Shijiazhuang, Jinan, Zhangqiu;
South-China: Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Dongguan, Zhongshan, Foshan, Fuzhou, Xiamen;
Central-China: Zaoyang.

FCL-cargo is being shipped from container terminals in Wuhan, Suzhou, Zhengzhou, Chongqing, Changsha, Yiwu, Chengdu.
