Insurance

Insurance - of goods is a complex of procedures which foresee insurance payments in an amount of partial or full indemnity if there is any injury to casualty of legal body or private person as result of loss, damage or miss of cargo independent of transportation mode.

Insurance of goods and transport

We are always interested in a long-term and mutually beneficial co-operation with our customers.

Entrusting us with your cargo you can be sure that our professionalism will safeguard it on the whole delivery route. However there are conditions and factors out of our influence which can offense the cargo safety. Any shipment carries risk of damage or loss. This is precisely why we always recommend insuring your cargo.

Without question every carrier possesses liability insurance policy or Voluntary insurance of responsibility of International carrier (so called CMR-insurance according to the Convention on the Contract for the International Carrying of Goods by Road of 1956, bill of lading or air waybill).

We turn your attention that according to “Voluntary insurance of responsibility of International carrier” restituted are losses caused by carrier only. The cargo owner receives no compensation if cargo got lost or damaged not by the carrier. Amount of the insurance payout is subject to different international conventions (Warsaw Convention for the Unification of certain rules relating to international carriage by air dated 12.10. and Convention for the Unification of Certain Rules for International Carriage by Air dated 28.05.1999, Convention on the Contract for the International Carriage of Goods by Road (CMR Convention) dated 19.05.1956).

Do not confuse the CMR-insurance with insurance of goods. Namely insurance of goods issued additionally defends the interests of cargo owner.

We always recommend our customers to issue additional policy of additional insurance which guarantee full restitution of all possible losses and damages of cargo. We use services of leading insurance companies only what guarantee your financial safety in all possible situations. Our turnover with partner insurance companies allows us to offer competitive insurance rates depending on type of cargo, delivery route and means of transport.
